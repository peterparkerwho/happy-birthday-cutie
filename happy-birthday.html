JAWAHAR

HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Some stories don't need a script.
They just happen, and you feel everything.

Scene 01  ·  The Theatre

Dark theatre. Somewhere in the middle,
your hand found mine —
and that was the only thing
I remember. INT. THEATRE · NIGHT

Scene 02  ·  The Stairs

You were a step below —
and you still reached up for me
like I was exactly where you wanted to be.

Few moments. No filter.
Impossible to forget. EXT. STAIRS · GOLDEN HOUR

Scene 03  ·  Late Night

Those first conversations —
neither of us meant to stay up that long.
But the night just… kept going.
And somewhere between
3am and nothing making sense,
I was already gone. INT. SEPARATE ROOMS · SAME FEELING

Happy
Birthday Jawahar

May this year have plot twists that make you smile,
shots that take your breath away,
and someone in every scene who picks you.
